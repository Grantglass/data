Thank you for visiting the William Blake Archive.

We welcome any comments or suggestions you may have.

If you are writing with a specific question or request, we encourage you to first consult our list of Frequently Asked Questions.

If you are writing with a bug report, we encourage you to first confirm that it is not already documented in our list of Known Hazards.

If you are writing to request permission to reproduce an image or a transcription, please complete this form. We can only grant permission to reproduce Archive materials; please do not contact us regarding materials not present in the Archive. Requests to reproduce images or transcriptions will only be considered if submitted via this form.

Finally, please be certain to sign your comments with a valid return email address, included in the body -- and not just the header -- of your message; if you do not receive a reply to a question or comment, it's because we've been unable to contact you.
